Necromancer

A project developed simultaneously with my Master's Thesis, Dead End, over the course of a ten week term. The final submission for my graduate compositing course. A layered fully realized Photoshop painting was split and projected onto relatively simple geometry to produce the scene and character. The geometry was then modeled into a shape to match the images, and the character rigged and animated. The animated scene in maya was reintroduced and assembled in nuke for a final render. At some point I want to take another stab at this kind of composition in a game engine. The process was documented in this development blog.
