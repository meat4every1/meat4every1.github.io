What is Golgotha?

Project Golgotha is an ambitious independent game development project in the early stages of prototyping. The design ethos of this game are focused on procedural content development. The early project goals are focused on day one pipeline development to ensure that Golgotha can constantly and easily evolve and expand after a future launch.
