What is Golgotha?

Project Golgotha is an ambitious independent game development project in the early stages of prototyping. The design ethos of this game are focused on procedural content development. The early project goals are focused on day one pipeline development to ensure that Golgotha can constantly and easily evolve and expand after a future launch. Golgothaâ€™s first major milestone will be a small prototype that will be presented to the public as the centrepiece of a crowd funding presentation.

While it is too early to give an explanation of the specific gameplay intentions we have for Golgotha, we can say that the premise is heavily influenced by the Dark Souls and Binding of Isaac series.

Early Concept Art (2013-2014)

At the onset there was a strong plan for our design philosophy. The goal is to create a powerful dark atmosphere that adds to our core themes of overcoming the terror of the unknown to embrace the thrill of discovery. At the same time our art style is somewhat filtered by more cartoony and lighthearted elements pulled from the design sense of hiyao miyazaki, Windwaker, Over The Garden Wall, etc.
